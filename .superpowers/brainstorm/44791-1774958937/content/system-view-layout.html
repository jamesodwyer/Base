C. Tree Sidebar + Detail Panel

File-explorer style tree on the left (grouped by tier/category). Click a group to see its tokens in a card grid on the right, with color previews and reference chains. A mini graph at the bottom shows the selected token's connections. Best for structured browsing.

You can also combine these — e.g., tree sidebar as default, with a "graph view" toggle. Pick the one that feels like the right starting point.
